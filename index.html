Applications

Transport

Our engine eliminates the need for a gearbox, enabling smoother control and higher power in a smaller size. Ideal for electric scooters, bicycles, trucks, and buses, with energy recuperation during braking.

Aviation

Reducing engine weight and size increases cargo capacity or flight time, critical for drones where weight is paramount.

Household Equipment

Our motors reduce starting current, making appliances like washing machines, refrigerators, and air conditioners quieter and more efficient.

Hand Electric Tools

Smaller, gearbox-free tools with controller-based speed and power for smoother, precise operation.

High Power Electric Motors

Soft-start technology eliminates the need for high starting currents, preventing emergency power disconnections in industrial settings.

Green Energy Generators

Our motors act as low-speed generators for wind and water energy, producing high-quality current without gearboxes or neodymium magnets, reducing costs and dependency on rare earth elements.
